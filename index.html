We are a class ‘A’ Electrical contractor, servicing the interior of British Columbia since 1984. With over 70 Employees and offices in Kamloops and Kelowna, we bring a high end and competitive edge to the construction market.

Tradition

Howell Electric has been serving the interior of British Columbia and beyond. Founded by Gordon Howell in 1984, Howell Electric benefits from its ‘family’ atmosphere. The electrical trade spans three generations of the Howell family, bringing young and old blood together, combining technical advantages, skills, and precision.

Experience

As a licensed, bonded, and insured electrical contractor with over 70 employees, Howell Electric brings quality and exceptional knowledge to the marketplace. Our management team boasts over 125 years of combined experience in the electrical industry.

Tradition

Not just a goal, but a standard. From the first year apprentice to the seasoned foreman, each individual is encouraged to strive for their best. Our employees recognize the fact that high standards are ‘good for business’ and that’s good for everyone.
